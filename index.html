Best Asian Recipes List

Asian Recipes

In this article you will be reading and can fine some of the best asian recipes in my opinion. Now be aware that some of these foods will be spicy.

Now another thing to note is that this is after all, opinion based to my favorite dishes, so they might not be everyones favorite however they are mine!

So now on to the juicy part of this list, lets start with the best asian dishes in my opinion.

    1. Easy Lo Mein

First on the list we have Lo Mein, a simple yet delicious meal. What I love about this meal is how versataile and easy it is to cook, it takes about 15 minutes!

Let alone how filling it is you can really add anything from tofu, beef, and veggies into this meal, and have an amazing filling meal in again, 15 minutes.

    2. Crispy Pork Belly

Here we have another beautiful dish, which is the pork belly. The thing I love most about the pork belly if done right, it is a super juicy savory meal.

Easily add lo mein or rice, even crab ragoons to finish the entire meal, and have an absolute delicious filling meal!

If you are a savory pork lover this dish is just for you, with a crispy outer layer and juicy tender inner, you just cant go wrong.

    3 Fire Cracker Chicken

At the finale choice of the best asian food I would consider firecracker-chicken taking the third spot.

This crispy, sweet, yet spicy dish brings perfection to a different level, not fried but browned until crispy, it gives crispy a new defenition.

packed with a load of flavor and a spicy punch. Personally I enjoy this dish because I dont have the highest tolerance for spicy food.

However, wth the mixes of sweet and spicy, this becomes a really tasty dish, serve it with rice, Lo Mein, or even steamed vegetables, or just by itself.
